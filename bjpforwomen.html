When we talk about India's first woman doctor, the name Dr. Anandibai Joshi naturally comes to mind. At a time when women had to struggle even for basic education, Anandibai pursued a medical degree and earned the honor of becoming a doctor. The revolutionary step she took for the upliftment of women has empowered women not only in the field of medicine but across various domains. The initiative "Aata Mazi Pali" is intended to be as joyful as Anandibai’s legacy. The aim of this campaign is for both women and men to embrace menstruation and promote it with the same joy. Like Dr. Anandibai, who went to great lengths for women’s health, all women should now come out of the shadows and proudly say, "Aata Mazi Pali!"
