Sneha Dubey

MLA, Vasai | Advocate for Progress

Sneha Dubey, the MLA of Vasai, is a leader driven by her vision for sustainable development and the betterment of her constituency. Her work reflects her dedication to improving the lives of Vasai’s residents through impactful policies and initiatives.

Sneha Dubey has championed numerous developmental projects in Vasai, focusing on infrastructure, education, healthcare, and environmental conservation. As a proactive leader, she has initiated community programs, addressed local concerns, and ensured the voices of her constituents are heard at higher levels of governance.

Her approach to leadership emphasizes inclusivity and innovation. Sneha actively engages with the youth, encourages local businesses, and prioritizes sustainability in all her efforts, making Vasai a model constituency.
